ABOUT-US


Ofform3D is the first digital fashion studio in Prague. Our mission is to bring more awareness to how the garment industry can benefit by going digital.


In this library, you can find more than 250 patterns in different formats that are compatible with all the main 3D software used in the fashion industry by pattern makers and designers. The use of these patterns can save you an enormous amount of time from the start of the design process until your final prototype.


The 3D visualization of clothing carries pattern, technical information, as well as the actual appearance of clothing. Such a representation reflects the reality of the actual appearance of the garment better than a sketch or illustrator drawing. It creates a new, more efficient way of communication between the designers and manufacturers.
